Gambling

The addiction

Gambling addiction is an issue that has affected millions of people from all walks of life, destroying their home lives and even proving deadly in some cases. Gambling finds its roots as a form of competition, and it could be this form of competition that causes the aggression associated with gambling addiction. Addicts get a sort of ruch of excitement out of gambling, and it is this rush that causes them to continue to gable, even if they have nothing left to wager, the stakes of the competition and the excitement of the randomness of it all is an addiction that the addict can't quite get from anything else. They develop an obsession, and will lash out and become aggressive when people try to intervene. the addict will try to find a way to make a bet or stakes at any time, and anywhere.
