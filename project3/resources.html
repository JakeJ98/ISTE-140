Summary

My website is about the interactions of gaming and gambling, how they synergize together, how they affect players, and more specifically children. My goal was to express what methods companies use to incoprporate gambling into games, how it can cause addiction, the affects of the addiction, and hwo it affects younger players. I wanted to provide information about how these practices began, and what they do. I also took the time to address some of the major controveries and what came from them.

Overall i feel like the website came out well, and Im satisfied with how it turned out. Im most proud of how the grid css came out, the placement of my images, and the media queries to resize all the images for a smaller screen. the two things I belive could use improvement are the size of the embeded videos and the navigation bar.
